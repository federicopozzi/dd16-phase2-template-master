Research question

How do Instagram stereotypes on touristic places influence their digital representation on Tripadvisor?

“Wait, I’ve seen that picture before! It’s trending on social media!” Instagram ha s rapidly and steadily changed the way we perceive places and choose our destinations. We identified one of the biggest stereotypical Instagram travel trend and analyze it. Could the beginning of a simple photographic trend boost tourism so much so that people travel to the place just be part of an iconic location?
